Flood Alert: Moderate Rainfall Expected in Low-Lying Areas

MDRRMO Operations Center

Disaster Management Unit

The MDRRMO has issued a flood alert for low-lying areas across the region due to expected moderate rainfall over the next 48 hours. Residents in flood-prone zones are advised to take precautionary measures and stay informed through official channels.

Weather monitoring systems indicate a convergence of weather systems that will bring sustained rainfall of 15-25mm per hour, particularly affecting areas near waterways and drainage basins. The alert is effective from 6:00 AM today until further notice.

Affected Areas

  • Barangay San Roque (Low-lying areas near river)
  • Barangay Sta. Cruz (Drainage basin zone)
  • Barangay Sto. Ni√±o (Coastal floodplain)
  • Barangay Bagumbayan (Urban drainage area)

Residents are reminded to prepare emergency kits and identify evacuation routes. Elderly residents and those with mobility challenges should make arrangements with family or neighbors for immediate evacuation if needed.

- MDRRMO Administrator

Precautionary Measures

For Residents

  • Stay updated through official announcements
  • Prepare emergency supply kits
  • Secure loose outdoor items
  • Identify evacuation centers

Emergency Contacts

  • MDRRMO Hotline: (02) 123-4567
  • Fire Department: 160
  • Ambulance: 161
  • Police: 166

All barangay disaster risk reduction and management councils are on alert status. Evacuation centers have been prepped and are ready to accommodate residents if necessary. The public is advised to avoid unnecessary travel during peak rainfall hours.

Monitoring and Updates

Our monitoring teams are deployed in critical areas and will provide hourly updates through our official social media channels and radio stations. Updates will also be coordinated with local barangay officials for immediate dissemination to residents.
